About Me

My goal is to see ugly, un-useful, and un-usable product UI's go away. I love taking on challenges, fighting the status quo within an industry by making applications once known as hard to use become simple and beautiful.

Growing up in New York City, I am hyper aware of the difference between beautiful useful things and just simply beautiful things. Sometimes, something is beautiful like the art in a gallery in Manhattan, but not super useful to the masses. Versus, the New York City subway map which is beautiful, easy to use, and useful. I strive to make every experience I design a reflection of this principle that being beautiful is not the only thing, the product also has to work beautifully, simply, and flawlessly.
